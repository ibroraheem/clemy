Our Projects

POS Installation and Management

Our team successfully managed the installation and ongoing maintenance of point-of-sale (POS) systems for major retail chains across Atlanta, including Walmart. This involved overseeing the deployment of self-checkout systems, ensuring smooth operation, and providing timely support for any technical issues that arose. Additionally, we handled data management tasks related to POS transactions, working with NCR's POS data re-entry network systems to ensure accuracy and efficiency.

Software Development

Our software development efforts have been instrumental in driving innovation in various sectors, particularly in education. We collaborated with partners to develop the Brighter Brain Application, an interactive learning platform designed to enhance remote education for students of all ages. Additionally, we contributed to the development of educational applications tailored to the specific needs of institutions, facilitating seamless communication and collaboration in virtual learning environments.

Network Solutions

Our expertise in network solutions has enabled us to support businesses in optimizing their infrastructure for maximum performance and reliability. For instance, we conducted a comprehensive switch and server refresh for Orally Auto Part, a leading automotive retailer in Atlanta, Georgia. This involved upgrading their network infrastructure to improve efficiency, security, and scalability, ensuring seamless operations across their various locations.

Network Solutions
